About Phong Cao

Welcome to my personal website, where you can find out pretty much everything about me, a really-geeky-but-not-very-nerdy Vietnamese dude!! =))


My full name is Phong Viet Cao. I'm a Vietnamese student currently attending Stony Brook University as an undergraduate. I major in Computer Science. I am most proficient with C/C++ and Python. I enjoy writing small, efficient and cross-platform programs using open-source technologies.


At present, I am maintaining Dingo, my very first personal open-source software. Dingo is a GStreamer-based audio/video manager and player that is coded heavily in C/C++ and customized for Linux operating system. Dingo 0.5.0, the first stable version of the software, was released on December 30, 2012.
